Why Property Investment in Kenya is on the Rise

Published on June 26, 2025 by Zia Property Insights

In recent years, Kenya's real estate market has experienced a noticeable uptrend. From urban expansion to infrastructure investments, many factors contribute to the growth of this sector.

Property investors are particularly attracted to areas like Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and the coastal belt. The demand for residential units, office space, and serviced apartments continues to grow, offering profitable returns for stakeholders.
